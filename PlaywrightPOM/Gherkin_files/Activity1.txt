//Activity 1
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
//Positive Secnario

Feature: Buying a product 
Secnario: Adding a product to cart on Saucedemo App
Given User is logged in
And user has chosen a product
And user clicks on add to cart button for the product
Then the product is added to cart

//Negative Secnario

Feature: Buying a product
Secnario: Adding a product to cart on Saucedemo App
Giver user is logged in
And user has chosen a product
But user has not clicked on add to cart button for the product
Then the product is not added to cart


//Activity 2
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
//Positive Secnario

Feature: Buying a product
Secnario: Removing a product from cart
Given user has added products to cart
And user chose the product to remove from cart
And user clicks on remove from cart button of that product
Then the product is removed from the cart

//Negative Secnario

Feature: Buying a product
Secnario: Removing a product from cart
Given user has not added products in cart
But user tries to click on remove from cart button
Then no products can be removed from cart
